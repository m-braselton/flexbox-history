I was born in Atlanta Georgia and lived there about 3 years. By the time I finished high school I had attended 10 different schools and lived in 5 different states. When I was 19, I found my home in the Sierra Nevada mountains. I have worked in Yosemite for 40 years now.

I started college in my late thirties. That is where I met my phillipina wife Rose. We have travelled to the Phillipines numerous times and have recently purchased a beautiful house there. Outside of Yosemite we have 10 acres with a house on top of a small mountain.

I love to take on new challenges and learn new skills. Living in the mountains, I have learn to be my own plumber, electrician, and carpenter. I also own and run a small saw mill. At age 50, I learned to fly a paraglider and have now logged over 500 hours in the air!
